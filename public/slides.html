The Problem

Solana traders are flying blind

  • 50,000+ new tokens launch on Solana every week
  • No reliable way to evaluate trust before trading
  • Existing tools check code — but ignore who deployed it and who holds it
  • Result: rug pulls, Sybil-farmed holders, and lost capital
The Solution

On-chain reputation as the trust layer

TokenTrust turns FairScale reputation into actionable intelligence for every token on Solana.

Deployer Scoring

Every token's deployer is scored via FairScale. A Platinum deployer is fundamentally different from a fresh wallet.

Holder Quality

Top holders are individually scored. High-reputation holders signal genuine demand, not Sybil farming.

Risk Detection

15+ risk patterns: concentrated supply, connected wallets, low deployer scores, missing authority revocations.
